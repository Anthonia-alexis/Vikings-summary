TONIA'S SUMMARY ON VIKINGS

I have just recently completed this series called Vikings.
Vikings is inspired by the sagas of Ragnar Lodbrok, a Viking who is one of the best-known legendary Norse heroes and notorious as the scourge of Anglo-Saxon England and West Francia. The show portrays Ragnar as a farmer from the Kattegat who rises to fame by raiding England and eventually becomes a Scandinavian king, with the support of his family and fellow warriors. In the later seasons, the series follows the fortunes of his sons and their adventures in England, Scandinavia, Kievan Rus', the Mediterranean and North America.

Travis Fimmel as RAGNAR

Ragnar is married to the popular and most powerful shield-maiden in the world, Lagertha.
They have a son called, Bjorn, who is extremely eager to join his father in raiding, but the young boy is only 13 years as at the time season 1 starts. Ragnar has a brother called, Rollo, who betrays him several times, despite that, Ragnar kept forgiving him.

As the series progresses, we see Ragnar raiding distant lands and coming back to tgheir home town in Norway called Kattegat.
Ragnar's bestfriend and boat builder, Floki ventures on all of Ragnar's adventures along with other Vikings and Shield Maidens. The sons of Ragnar all grow up to be great men and even outdo their father in feats.
